Chapter 1 General Provisions
Article 1: The Cyber Republic of China grants the Hou Kuo-style socialism for the people, governed by the people, and enjoyed by the people as a democratic republic.
Article 2: The sovereignty of the Cyber Republic of China belongs to all the people.
Chapter 2: The Rights and Duties of Citizens
Article 3: Anyone who joins the archives of the Chinese Cyber Republic is considered a citizen of the Chinese Cyber Republic.
Article 4: Citizens of the Chinese Cyber Republic are equal before the law.
The state respects and protects human rights.
Every citizen enjoys the rights stipulated by the Constitution and the law, and at the same time must fulfill their obligations.
Chapter 3: State Institutions
Article 4: The National People's Congress of the Republic of China on the Internet is the highest state authority. Its permanent body is the National People's Congress.
Article 5: The National People's Congress and its Standing Committee shall, in accordance with the law and regulations, exercise the state's legislative and electoral powers, and shall elect the President of the Republic of China on the Internet, the Chairman of the Central Military Commission, and the Premier of the State Council.
Article 6: The State Council of the Republic of China on the Internet, that is, the Central People's Government, is the executive organ of the highest state authority and the highest administrative organ of the state.
Article 7: The Chairman of the Central Military Commission is accountable to the National People's Congress, the National People's Congress of Chinese Citizens, and the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.
Chapter Four: National Flag, National Anthem, National Emblem, and Capital
Article 8: The national flag of the Cyber Republic of China is the 'Ya' character flag.
Article 9: The national emblem of the Cyber Republic of China features a Minecraft grass block in the center, with five five-pointed stars surrounding it on the left.
Article 10: The capital of the Cyber Republic of China is Chairman Hou City.
