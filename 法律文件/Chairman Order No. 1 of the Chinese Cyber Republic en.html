Chairman Order No. 1 of the Chinese Cyber Republic

Number One

The "Constitution of the Cyber Republic of China" has been passed by the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the Cyber Republic of China and is hereby promulgated, to take effect from the date of its promulgation.

President of the Cyber Republic of China, Hou Kuo
December 14, 2025
