Organic Law of the National People's Congress of the Cyber Republic of China

(Adopted by the First National People's Congress on February 28, 2026; signed by the President of the Cyber Republic of China on February 28, 2026, and promulgated by the Office of the President of the Cyber Republic of China)

Article 1: In order to implement socialism with Houkuo characteristics and uphold the nature of the state as being under the rule of the people, this law is formulated in accordance with the Constitution.
Article 2: The People's Congresses are divided into two levels: the National People's Congress and the municipal-level People's Congresses.
Article 3: Each level of the People's Congress manages the administrative areas under its jurisdiction, and based on the specific conditions of the subordinate administrative areas, coordinates independently and formulates relevant laws and regulations, serving the people wholeheartedly.
Article 4: Each level of the People's Congress democratically elects representatives of the People's Congress for its administrative area, and periodically convenes meetings integrated with the specific situation. Representatives at each level participate in the corresponding People's Congress.
Article 5: The People's Congress implements the system of democratic centralism. When a representative submits a proposal, it is voted on by all representatives. If the votes are in majority, the proposal passes; if tied, it is decided by the State Chairman; if it does not reach a majority, the proposal fails.
Article 6: Governments at all levels and other state organs within the subordinate administrative areas shall cooperate with the resolutions of the People's Congress and report their work to all representatives when the People's Congress convenes. The process should be fair and transparent.
Article 7: This law shall come into force from the date of promulgation.
